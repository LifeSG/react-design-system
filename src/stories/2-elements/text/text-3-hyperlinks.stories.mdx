## Hyperlinks

There are 2 variants for hyperlinks and their sizes distinguish them.

### Default

<Preview>
    <Text.Hyperlink.Default href="https://www.google.com" target="blank">
        Click me
    </Text.Hyperlink.Default>
</Preview>

### Small

<Preview>
    <Text.Hyperlink.Small href="https://www.google.com" target="blank">
        Click me
    </Text.Hyperlink.Small>
</Preview>

### External Links

As we recommend providing an indicator that the link is leading to an external site, you can specify the `external`
prop to be `true`.

<Preview>
    <Text.Hyperlink.Default
        external={true}
        href="https://www.google.com"
        target="blank"
    >
        Click me
    </Text.Hyperlink.Default>
</Preview>
