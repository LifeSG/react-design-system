<Secondary>Overview</Secondary>

The primary call to action component.

```tsx

```

<Heading3>Advanced styling</Heading3>

If you require text of a bolder weight, you can make use of the `<strong>` tag.

<Canvas>
    <Story name="With bold text">
        <Alert type="success">
            <p>
                You can add <strong>bold text</strong> to signify or highlight
                certain information. Or perhaps even add a&nbsp;
                <a href="https://life.gov.sg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">
                    hyperlink
                </a>
                &nbsp;to direct users to some external source.
            </p>
        </Alert>
    </Story>
</Canvas>

Or if you require a custom display, you can construct your layout and pass it into the component.

<Canvas>
    <Story name="With custom display">
